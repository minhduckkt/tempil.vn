Product Description

Tempilaq® Advanced 650°F — Liquid form, applications & standards

Brush onto metal surface before PWHT to confirm uniform temperature across the entire weld zone. Especially useful for rapid multi-point inspection on large pressure vessels. 2oz bottle — compact and easy to carry on site.

After application the solvent evaporates quickly (quick-dry), leaving a thin film on the steel. When the temperature reaches 650°F (343°C), the film changes colour clearly — a visual result recordable by photo for QA/QC records. Thinner (Part #24597) may be used for dilution if needed.

When should Tempilaq® be used instead of a crayon?

Tempilaq® Advanced vs Tempilstik® — Choosing the right tool

💧 Tempilaq® Advanced — Use when:
  • Curved surfaces, small-diameter pipe
  • Blind angles, hard to reach by hand
  • Wide, uniform surface coverage needed
  • Verify uniform heat distribution in PWHT furnace
  • Surfaces with ridges, grooves or complex profiles
🖊️ Tempilstik® — Use when:
  • Flat, easily accessible surfaces
  • Quick spot-check at a specific point
  • Outdoor / windy site conditions
  • Immediate reading needed, no drying wait
  • Multiple temperature levels required simultaneously

🖌️ Correct application technique

Application procedure and reading results

Shake the bottle well. Apply a thin, even coat on clean steel (free of oil and grease). Allow the solvent to evaporate fully (30–60 s depending on conditions). Heat the surface — when it reaches 650°F (343°C) the film changes colour clearly. Photograph before the surface cools. If Tempilaq® thickens from evaporation, use Thinner Part #24597 to restore viscosity.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Usage

Can Tempilaq® 650°F be used outdoors?

Yes, but avoid strong wind during application so the film dries evenly. In high-ambient-temperature conditions (>40°C / 104°F), the solvent evaporates faster — apply immediately before heating. In high-wind offshore conditions, a Tempilstik® crayon is usually more practical for quick spot-checks.

❓ Storage

How should Tempilaq® be stored?

Seal tightly after every use — the solvent evaporates and thickens Tempilaq® if left open. Store in a cool, ventilated place away from heat and direct sunlight. If too thick, use Thinner Part #24597 to restore original viscosity.
