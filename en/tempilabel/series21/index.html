Product features

Why choose Tempilabel® Series 21?

  • Single-level label — each label indicates one temperature threshold for an instant reading
  • Irreversible — permanent color change once the threshold is reached, creating a tamper-proof inspection record
  • Self-adhesive, applied directly to the equipment surface — no tools, no power required
  • ±2% °F accuracy — suited to QC of motors, bearings, electrical equipment and piping
  • Two formats: packs of 210 labels (small projects) and rolls of 1000 labels (high-volume production)
  • Genuine import — includes U.S. certificate of origin (C/O), C/Q quality certificate and full VAT
