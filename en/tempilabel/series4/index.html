Series 4 vs Series 21 — Which to use when?

Series 4: A single label with 4 consecutive temperature levels — ideal for continuous monitoring and spotting a rising-temperature trend on running equipment (motors, pumps, bearings). More economical when several thresholds must be tracked.

Series 21: A single-level label — use it to confirm one specific threshold precisely (e.g. PWHT reaching 300°F) or when you need high volumes in rolls of 1000 labels.

Product features

Why choose Tempilabel® Series 4?

  • 4 ascending temperature windows on one label — catch an overheating trend early
  • Irreversible — windows change color permanently, cannot be reset, creating a tamper-proof record
  • Pressure-sensitive self-adhesive — installs in seconds, no tools or power required
  • Replaces costly temperature sensors for continuous-monitoring applications
  • Fits condition-based maintenance (CBM) standards
  • Includes U.S. certificate of origin (C/O), C/Q quality certificate and full VAT invoice
